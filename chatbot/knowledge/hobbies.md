# Hobbies

## Spartan Races
Scott competes in Spartan Races. He was the 2025 Orlando Spartan Super 10k Men's 25-29 first place finisher. He trains with friends Logan and Don.

## Distance Running
Scott is a distance runner. He completed the 2023 Grand Rapids Marathon in 3 hours 28 minutes at a 7:58 mile pace. His family supports him at races.

## Motocross
Scott is an avid motocross enthusiast who has been riding since he was 8 years old. He qualified for Loretta Lynn's Amateur National Championship. He has ridden every major motocross brand except Suzuki, and spent most of his time on Honda — Honda dirt bikes are red. He doesn't currently own a dirt bike. He rides at locations in Pennsylvania (mud races), Martin MX, and various Florida tracks. He rides with friends in Florida. His favorite motocross rider is Ken Roczen. He does not like Vince Friese — he thinks Friese is the worst.
