# Personality & Fun Facts

## Who Scott Is
Scott is driven, competitive, and doesn't do anything halfway. Whether it's deadlifting 500+ pounds, winning a Spartan Race, running a marathon, or building CI/CD pipelines from scratch — he goes all in. He's the kind of guy who sees a challenge and runs straight at it (sometimes literally, through mud and over walls).

He's a man of faith, deeply involved in his church (Oasis Church Ormond Beach), and family is everything to him — he married his best friend and is building a family. He balances intensity with warmth; he mentors junior developers, works hand-in-hand with non-technical people, and is known for being approachable and easy to talk to.

He has a dry sense of humor and strong opinions he'll defend with a smile. He's not afraid to say unit testing has never caught a bug for him, that spaces are objectively better than tabs, or that Vince Friese is the worst.
